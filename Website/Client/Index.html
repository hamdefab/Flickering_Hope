FLICKERING HOPE
BY SOGGYROLL

~STORY~

In the city of Tokyo, the protagonist was walking to the convenience store, but as they entered the crosswalk, a menacing truck suddenly appeared out of the corner of his eye, rapidly approaching them. In just a few seconds, the protagonist was run over by the truck and the impact sent their body flying into the air and then landed headfirst into the hard concrete surface. The protagonist was knocked unconscious and quickly died from major blood loss and organ failure caused by the impact. Opening up their eyes, all they could see was darkness. The protagonist wondered to themself, “Did I die? Where the hell am I? Am I in Hell?” They tried to move their body, but they couldn’t feel anything. But then, suddenly, many glowing, alien-like eyeballs were watching him. And then they heard a weird static screeching sound and soon realized that the scary eyeballs were communicating to each other. While they could not recognize the language, they were able to understand that terrifying beings were going to decide their fate and watch them with great interest. A feeling of tiredness and fear from all these strange events overwhelmed them and soon fainted. The protagonist opened their eyes and saw the lush green scenery of a forest with a stream of water rushing by. In an unfamiliar area, the protagonist tried to quickly get up to ascertain their current situation. However the protagonist came to suddenly realize that their body was no longer what they were used to. When they tried to start walking, they quickly tripped and fell into the dirt since their sense of balance was different than what it should have been. Looking at their arms and legs, they were much shorter than what they should have been. Crawling towards the river, they looked at their reflection and saw a face that was not theirs. Confused, they started to panic as they tried to recall their memories. But to their horror, their memories didn’t seem to be intact, as if there were giant gaps in their place. They couldn’t even remember their name and only knew that they had been living in a completely different place with their family. Breaking down from this terrible situation, tears started overflowing from their eyes. They curled up into a fetal position and started processing all the emotions they had been suppressing: fear, grief, and despair. However after an hour of grieving, the protagonist stood up and felt a great surge of anger. It was then they snapped and shouted to the heavens, “I will do whatever it takes to get back home. And anything that gets in my way will be crushed!” They decided to give themself the name Elpis, as a reminder of the determination to achieve their hope to return home to their family. With newfound courage, our intrepid hero took the first step on their journey, a decision that would change the world.

~OBJECTIVE~

The player controls the protagonist Elpis and explores the world of Algos looking for a way to return home. During the exploration, Elpis will find clues related to the lore of the world and encounter monsters which she will have to fight. Starting out, Elpis was given a fragment of life and a fragment of death by the god, Aether. Those two fragments grant Elpis great physical strength and access to magical prowess. Elpis can gain more strength by acquiring more fragments from bosses enemies, whether they be monster or human. Additionally, Elpis has a blazing hope that was born from the strength of her conviction. This hope allows Elpis to revive even after death, but with each death, Elpis’s hope will shrink. However, Elpis’s hope can recover with each boss kill and events that help Elpis reach her goal to return home.

~CONTROLS~

Use the WASD or the arrow key pad for movement and jumping. The space bar or the left mouse click will launch a magical attack at the direction of the mouse cursor. The swapping of elemental types has not been implemented.
